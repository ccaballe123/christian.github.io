TSM Dominating the Esports Scene

The organization known as Team Solo Mid has become a household name for various competitive gaming scenes. Andy Dinh's desire to legitimize his professional gaming career propelled his organization to acquire multiple teams across a multitude of games and essential sponsors. LoL, CoD, Super Smashbros, VainGlory, Csgo, and multiple other games have prospered under the growing organization. With hopes of acquiring new teams and sponsorships, Team Solo Mid shows no signs of slowing down.

Team Solo Mid's current pool of teams includes:

  • League of Legends
  • Super Smash Brothers Melee
  • Overwatch
  • Vainglory
  • Player Unknown BattleGrounds
